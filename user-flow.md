# User Flow

## First-Time User

1. Open GlowCast.
2. Tap `Upload Surface Photo` or take a photo.
3. App shows the surface.
4. Tap `Dummy AI Detect` or `Add Manual Zone`.
5. Tap zones to include/exclude.
6. Choose effect.
7. Export alignment guide.
8. Line projector up to the same spot.
9. Play final mapped animation.

## Ideal Future Flow

1. Open app.
2. Point camera at house/wall.
3. App suggests windows, doors, garage panels, and major projection areas.
4. User taps `Project only windows`, `Project around windows`, or `Custom`.
5. User picks animation pack.
6. App exports or projects.
