# GlowCast Product Plan

## Product Category

Consumer-friendly projection mapping app.

## Target Position

Projection mapping made easy for people who do not want professional software.

## Ideal User

- Halloween home decorator
- Christmas light/projection decorator
- small business/storefront owner
- DJ or small band
- church/event team
- wedding/event decorator
- trade show booth owner
- school/theater volunteer

## Core Promise

Point at a wall or house, mark what matters, pick an effect, and project.

## Differentiator

The killer feature is not just projection mapping. It is AI-assisted masking.

The app suggests areas like windows, doors, garage panels, signs, trim, arches, and rectangles. The user can project only on those areas, around those areas, or manually adjust everything.

## Product Modes

### Mode 1: Direct Projection

Phone connects directly to projector.

Best for quick use.

### Mode 2: Export Package

Phone/web app exports:

- MP4 loop
- alignment guide PNG
- optional mask PNG
- optional project file

Best for reliability and compatibility.

## Free Version

- watermark
- limited templates
- limited export quality
- basic mapping tools

## Paid Revenue

- remove watermark
- premium export quality
- Halloween pack
- Christmas pack
- Business neon pack
- Concert pack
- Wedding/event pack
- logo reveal pack

## Product Rule

Keep it simple. Do not become pro software too early.
